Privacy Policy

At Zhongbang Spinning Industrial Co., Ltd. (hereinafter, “Zhongbang”), with operational headquarters in Shenzhen and Dongguan, China, we are committed to protecting the information that our clients and business partners share with us during the development of commercial relationships, manufacturing projects, technical production, and export services.

This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect the information we receive.

1. Information We Collect

Zhongbang only collects information necessary for business and commercial purposes, including:

  • Corporate contact information (company name, representative’s name, position, corporate email, company phone number).
  • Information related to orders, technical specifications, commercial documentation, logistics details, and data required for production and shipping.
  • Data related to billing, payments, and contractual compliance.
  • Information submitted through our contact forms or email communications.

We do not request or process sensitive personal data.

2. Purpose of Data Processing

The information collected is used exclusively to:

  • Establish and manage business relationships.
  • Process requests for information, quotations, and orders.
  • Develop, produce, and deliver products according to the customer’s specifications.
  • Send updates regarding orders, logistics processes, or technical support.
  • Fulfill contractual, commercial, and legal obligations applicable to international export and import.

3. Data Transfer and Storage

We may share information only with:

  • Internal production and quality teams.
  • Approved suppliers required to carry out manufacturing processes.
  • Logistics and transportation operators necessary for export.
  • Communication or storage platforms used by the company.

All information is securely stored in internal systems located in China and/or on external provider servers that comply with international security standards.

4. Data Protection

Zhongbang applies technical and administrative measures to safeguard the business information entrusted to us, including:

  • Controlled and limited access for authorized personnel only.
  • Security protocols in our facilities and systems.
  • Confidential handling of documents and communications.
  • Compliance processes based on international quality and management standards.

5. Rights of the Information Owner

Corporate clients may request at any time:

  • Access to the information we have stored.
  • Updates or corrections to data.
  • Deletion of information when legally applicable.
  • Limitation of the use of non-essential information.

To exercise these rights, you may contact us through the company’s official email.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

Zhongbang may update this notice at any time. The most recent version will always be available on our website.
